About Botonoids 2008

Botonoids 2008 is the followup to the original Botonoids that was released in 2004. Botonoids 2008 retains the classic gameplay of the original Botonoids, with the addition of many new features!

Download

Download Botonoids 2008!

New Features

  • New and greatly improved graphics.
  • New item system.
  • Three player mode.
  • Support for gamepads and other input devices.
  • Customizable map size and game duration.
  • Player Handicaps
  • New Botonoid characters!
  • New music and sounds.
  • Real time rendered particle effects.
  • Higher video resolution.
