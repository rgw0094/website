Fallingwater

fallingwater.org/


Fallingwater is an iconic home designed by Frank Lloyd Wright. It is often considered to be his greatest masterpiece, and one of the best works of American architecture.


Located just down the road from our wedding venue, we highly recommend visiting if you have the time! Tours are given between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. daily, except Wednesdays. Tour tickets can be bought on-site or online.

Ohiopyle State Park


Ohiopyle features a plethora of outdoor activities, including whitewater rafting, rock climbing, fishing, and golf. It's also a fun word to say!


The Great Allegheny Passage passes through the area, making it a great location for hiking and biking.
